Education in China is calling on us to make a change. While students with so-called 'background' more likely to obtain the top tier of what is called the 'elite' of Chinese education system, ordinary people, especially those from the underdeveloped places, deserve to access much more than they have now.

Anhui is what I call an 'embarrassing' province. Unlike Shanghai, Beijing, Guangdong and Jiangsu, it is not really rich. Compared to our eastern neighbours (Jiangsu, Zhejiang and Shanghai), we actually look like poor guys. Sometimes we are even defined as 'born-to-be thieves' by people from rich regions. Also unlike poorer regions, like Xinjiang and Qinghai, we seem to be living in fortune. And this is the reason why we are not recieving aids as many as these regions. Although things are getting much, much more better these days, I still want to ask: is this what we deserve?

I am not here to complain about inequality. Absolute equity is utterly a distant dream. But by working hard, we can prove that we are not less intelligent than our eastern neighbours. We are just waiting for the opportunities.

Be a proud Anhui citizen and be myself.
